About Me

I am a loving mother, sister and daughter. I served in the U.S Navy for 8 years. During my time in the Navy I was a military police (MP). I come from a family of service members; my father, uncle, brother and others served in different branches of the service but we all built our lives off serving the country. I have lived in many places two of my favorites are Okinawa, Japan and Manama, Bahrain. I separated from the Navy in 2021 to persue my degree and career in cybersecurity, which I am currently a student at Kennesaw State University.

This website is for a web development class project for Kennesaw State University.
